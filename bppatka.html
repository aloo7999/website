BULLET PROOF HEAD PROTECTION

SHIELD ARMOUR BULLET PROOF HEAD PROTECTION (PATKA/HEAD GEAR) has a Ballistic strip manufactured from a Die Pressed PHANTOM STEEL with high density Cushion material used as inner lining . It provides protection against fragmentation munitions and low velocity Bullets. It does not cause any ill effects like fatique, headache to the wearer during prolong usage. It has been specially designed for turbaned security personnel to provide vital Head Protection. It comes in 3 different sizes. Furthermore the chin strap & specially designed straps at the rear ensure a perfect head grip.

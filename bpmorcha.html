The practical concept of easily manageable Bullet Proof Secure Picket (Naka) in three parts for easy carriage, is the concept behind this extremely useful aid to security Agencies. This versatile equipment has multifarious utilities as a readymade completely secure picket for all kinds of moblie as well as Static duties in terrorist infested or any other onerous situations. Particularly for difficult terrains like J&K, Andhra, North Eastern Sector, etc. this will prove to be boon to security agencies & will provide the necessary cutting edge.
